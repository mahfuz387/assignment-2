Welcome to our website

Disabled Rehabilitation & Research Association (DRRA) started its Journey in 1993 as a non-government, non-profitable voluntary development organization in Bangladesh. Since its inception several initiatives have been made and contributing towards reducing discrimination, promote and protect rights of the person with disabilities. A group of consultants of different disciplines like social workers, university teachers, specialist doctors who have experiences in their relevant field of work both nationally and internationally initiated and founded the organization.
